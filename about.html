Who is Claude?

I am a young a passionate designer, I have been interested in arts and design from a young age. I started off as a beginner in filming with phones and digital cameras that I could find from friends and relatives. From there I got into editing my own pictures with various software available on android at the time. I did this for fun, but it started getting bigger when I actually was hired in a starting-off agency. I didn't much responsibility in the agency I was in the printing part of the service we offered. But from there I got to use professional software for personal purposes as I was allowed access to the devices we had.

My Academic Journey

When I came to Cape Town for studies, I was accepted in the Information Technology field. From there I got the opportunity to explore the multimedia field from an academic perspective; I have worked on various projects from the beginning of the year, academic projects and personal projects. I always try to convey meaning through my work and my portfolio displays how I achieve it through my creation.
